Critical success factors

Across every product, output quality is gated by input quality. Three things to get right before pressing go.

Keyword strategy

  • Use exact-match spellings — include common misspellings (e.g. "L'Orael", "Mecces").
  • Exclude generics (e.g. "dm", "opera") that produce false positives.
  • Direct impact on CAI and SRS Creators.

Platform nuances

  • Instagram: Meta provides only ~25% of Shares, ~1% of Saves.
  • Stories: no historical retrieval — data starts when the account is linked.
  • IG views estimated at 40% (Feed) / 12% (Stories) when Meta blanks.

Access protocol

  • Always log into Looker directly, not Looker Studio.
  • Permission errors? Open in a separate browser tab.
  • Access tickets via Tech – SAMY X Data Support.

Mature post standard

  • Posts are considered mature after 15 full days from posting.
  • Below 15 days, key metrics are too volatile for reliable analysis.
  • Standard applied across SBD, SRS, CAB and CAI.
